Information Systems

In our Information Systems unit we are currently involved in a group project where each member of our members will have to contribute towards a group project which consists of developing a database for a bike rental company that is currently using an inefficient method on taking in information from its customers which includes orders, payments and their details like address etc. all of this information will be saved with his current paper based system but now he wants a computer based system where he can save and update information. During the development of this system we will need to work as a group to develop a report and then to design the database system which will then be followed up with a presentation, In the first screenshot you can see a Use Case Diagram which will highlight all the different roles in a business and what they will be doing, which is then followed up by an Entity Relationship Diagram which is another graphical representation of the diferent entities and their relatipship to each other, which will alow us to organise the data.
